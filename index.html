Quick answers

Built to be simple

Kairos is still growing, but the goal is clear: a free curriculum site that is easy for kids to use and easy for families to understand.

Does progress save?

Yes. The student profile, unlocked lessons, XP, scores, and mood save in the browser with local storage.

Is it free?

Yes. The site is designed to be free and accessible through GitHub Pages.

What happens after a lesson?

Students get a summary screen with score, accuracy, XP, mood, and question breakdown.

Can more grades be added?

Yes. The structure can grow into Grade 2, Grade 4, and more courses as the curriculum expands.
